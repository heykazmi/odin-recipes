Eggs and Bacon

What is Egg and Bacon?

A dish consisting of fried or grilled slices of bacon and one or more fried eggs. This is a classic breakfast dish that is popular in many cultures around the world. The salty bacon and the rich, runny eggs complement each other perfectly, and the dish is often served with toast, hash browns, and other breakfast staples.

How to make Eggs and Bacon?

Let's take your beef bacon and eggs breakfast to the next level with some inspiration from the fantastical culinary worlds of anime! Here are two fun and flavorful recipes:

This fiery dish channels the spirit of the Demon Slayer Corps with its bold flavors.


Ingredients:

  • 4 slices beef bacon, chopped
  • 1 red chili pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 cup sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon rice vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped scallions, for garnish (optional)

Steps:

  1. Fry the beef bacon until crispy. Drain the fat and set aside.
  2. Saute the chili pepper, onion, and garlic in the same pan until softened. Stir in the gochujang and cook for another minute.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, sesame oil, soy sauce, rice vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the egg mixture into the pan with the chili mixture and cook, stirring occasionally, until scrambled.
  5. Fold in the cooked beef bacon.
  6. Serve immediately with rice and garnish with chopped scallions (optional).

Tips:

  • Adjust the amount of chili pepper to your spice preference. Go easy, go bold, the choice is yours!
  • Want a richer flavor? Swap the sesame oil for bacon fat. Remember, with great flavor comes great responsibility (calories).
